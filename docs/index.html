How it works

You describe what happened in plain English. Nine AI agents analyse your situation and generate everything you need to get paid.

🔎

1. Parse your situation

The Case Analyst agent extracts platform, amount, currency, jurisdiction, issue type, and state from your free-text description. No forms to fill.

⚖️

2. Research the law

The Legal Researcher searches a built-in database of UK and US statutes, procedures, and platform-specific rules. Finds every law that protects you.

📉

3. Score your case

Case strength is scored 0-95 based on amount, evidence, jurisdiction options, applicable law, and known platform patterns.

📝

4. Draft documents

The Document Drafter generates ready-to-send legal documents — Letter Before Action, Demand Letter, GDPR SAR, BBB/FTC complaints — pre-filled with your details.

📅

5. Build your timeline

The Timeline Manager creates a day-by-day escalation plan with exact deadlines. Day 0: send LBA. Day 14: deadline expires. Day 21: file MCOL.

🎯

6. Track and escalate

Mark steps complete, add notes, skip actions. If they don't pay, GroundLevel tells you exactly where to escalate next.

Step-by-step guide

From download to sending your first legal letter in under 5 minutes.

1

Download the file

Click the download button above, or grab GroundLevel-v2.html from GitHub. Save it anywhere on your computer — Desktop, Documents, a USB stick. It's just one file.

2

Open in Chrome

Double-click the file to open it in your browser. No server needed. No internet needed. It works completely offline out of the box.

3

Pick your jurisdiction

Select UK, US, or Both on the landing page. This determines which laws and procedures are used in your analysis.

4

Describe what happened

Click "Describe what happened" and type your situation in plain English. Include: the platform name, how much they owe you, what happened, and where you're based. Example: "DataAnnotation owes me $2,400 for completed AI training work. They approved my tasks 6 weeks ago but haven't paid. I'm in London."

5

Click "Analyse My Case"

Watch the 9 agents fire one by one. In seconds you'll see your case strength score, applicable laws, your rights in plain English, and recommended actions.

6

Build your action plan

Click "Build My Action Plan" to go to your dashboard. You'll find ready-to-send legal documents (Letter Before Action, GDPR SAR, Demand Letter, BBB/FTC complaints), an evidence checklist, and a step-by-step escalation timeline.

7

Copy, download, and send

Click any document to view it. Hit "Copy to Clipboard" or "Download .txt" to save it. The documents include today's date, the platform name, the amount owed, and the correct legal citations. Send them.

8

Track your progress

Use the tracking screen to mark steps complete, skip actions, and add notes. Your entire case history is stored in your browser's IndexedDB — private, persistent, and exportable as JSON.
